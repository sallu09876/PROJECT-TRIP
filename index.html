Our Roots

At CloudWay, our story began with a shared dream — to let the world experience the heart and soul of God’s Own Country through meaningful travel. Founded by six passionate explorers, each with diverse travel experiences and deep roots in Kerala, we set out to create something more than just another holiday provider. As the best travel agency in Kerala, we specialize in crafting customized Kerala tour packages that go beyond the ordinary — blending culture, nature, and comfort in ways that truly connect with every traveller. Whether it’s a serene Ayurveda retreat in Kerala, a family-friendly backwater cruise, or a romantic escape through the hills of Munnar and Thekkady, our journeys are designed to awaken your senses and leave lasting footprints. With a legacy of excellence in travel and hospitality, CloudWay is more than a name — it’s your gateway to explore, reconnect, and discover destinations through stories, people, and moments that matter.
